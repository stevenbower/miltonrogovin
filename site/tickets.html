2009 Birthday Exhibition and Event Schedule

Through the Fall and Winter we have scheduled a series of events in celebration of the 100th birthday of Milton Rogovin. All events listed on the Exhibition and Event Schedule take place at the Burchfield Penney Art Center in Buffalo, New York.

September 2009 – January 3, 2010
From the Western Door to the Lower West Side, Photography by Milton Rogovin and Poetry by Eric Gansworth. An exhibition of poetry by Native American Eric Gansworth of Canisius College, Buffalo, NY, illustrated with Milton Rogovin’s photographs of Native Americans on Buffalo area reservations and the Lower West Side. The exhibit will be open through January 3, 2010.

For additional information about these events please contact the Burchfield Penney Art Center at 716-878-4529 or write to The Rogovin Collection.
