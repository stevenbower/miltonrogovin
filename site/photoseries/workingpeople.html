About This Series

"In 1975 I closed my optometric office in order to do a photo series in the steel mills of the Buffalo area (plus one series in Philadelphia). In this series, I tried, if possible, to film the workers both at work and at home with their families." –Milton Rogovin

Selected photographs shown here are from the following mills and factories:

  • Amherst Foundry (1978-87)
  • Atlas Steel Casting (1976-87)
  • Bethlehem Steel (1978-79)
  • Billy Penn (1978)
  • Buffalo Forge (1978)
  • Buffalo China Pottery (1978)
  • Chevrolet Motors (1977-78)
  • Dresser Foundry (1978)
  • Ford Stamping (1978)
  • Donna Hanna Furnace (1978-85)
  • Markel Electric (1979-80)
  • Republic Steel (1978-87)
  • Shenango Ingot Molds (1976-87)
  • Westinghouse (1979-80)
